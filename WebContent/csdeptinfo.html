Welcome to the Department of Computer Science. The department offers BS, MS, and PhD programs in Computer Science; MS programs in Software Engineering, Information Systems, and Information Security and Assurance; and a BS program in Applied Computer Science with various concentrations. The department also offers concentrations in the inter-disciplinary MS in Data Analytics Engineering and the PhD in Information Technology offered by the Volgenau School of Engineering.

  • MS Computer Science
    Required Courses:
    1. CS 583 - Analysis of Algorithms
    2. At least four courses (12 credits) must be chosen from the advanced courses in the list of preapproved courses from at least three different areas.
    3. At least six courses, including two advanced courses, must be designated CS.
    4. At least eight courses must be taken from the list of preapproved courses. Up to two computer science-related courses that are not on the list of preapproved courses may be taken with the approval of the Computer Science Department.

  • MS Information Systems
    Required Courses:
    1. INFS 612 Principles and Practice of Communication Networks
    2. INFS 622 Information Systems Analysis and Design
    3. INFS 614 OR CS 550 Database Management
    4. ISA 562 Information Security Theory and Practice

  • MS Software Engineering
    Required Courses:
    1. SWE 619 Object-Oriented Software Specification and Construction
    2. SWE 621 Software Modeling and Architectural Design
    3. SWE 622 Distributed Software Engineering
    4. SWE 637 Software Testing

  • MS Information Security and Assurance
    Required Courses:
    1. ISA 562 Information Security Theory and Practice
    2. ISA 656 Network Security
    3. INFS 612 Principles and Practice of Communication Networks OR CS 555 - Computer Communications and Networking
